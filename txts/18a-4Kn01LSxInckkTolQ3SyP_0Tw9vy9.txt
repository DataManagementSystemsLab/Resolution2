Resolution on Purchase of Scantron for Teaching Evaluations

WHEREAS,    The faculty desire quantitative evaluations of student
           perceptions of teaching,

THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED

      That the College will purchase a Scantron iNSIGHT 4ES optical mark
           recognition (OMR) and image scanner for use by the faculty to be
           housed in the Faculty Senate office.
