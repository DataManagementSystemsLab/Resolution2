Resolution regarding tuition increase for 2002-2003
WHEREAS:
The costs of delivering a quality college education to the students of New York State have risen at
a rate at least equivalent to that of the rate of inflation; and

WHEREAS:
The state of New York has a fiduciary and constitutional responsibility to support public higher

education for the benefit of its taxpayers, both present and future; and
WHEREAS:
Tuition has not been raised at the State University of New York since the 1995-96 academic year
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED:
That the College Senate of SUNY Fredonia calls on the Chancellor to support a modest tuition
increase for the 2002-2003 academic year.

Resolution Passed November 12, 2001 by SUNY Fredonia College Senate.
