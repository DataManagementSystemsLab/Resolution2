SUNY Old Westbury
Addendum to
University Faculty Senate
Guide for the Evaluation of Undergraduate Academic Programs
In addition to the criteria outlined in the University Faculty Senate Guide for the
Evaluation of Undergraduate Programs, Old Westbury programs should address
the following issues in their Five Year Reviews:
1) Changes in program curriculum or philosophy in the last 5 years (if any) and
their rationale.
2) The ratio between day and evening courses and summer offerings.
3) Contribution to general education.

4) Arrangements with other programs and two year schools.
