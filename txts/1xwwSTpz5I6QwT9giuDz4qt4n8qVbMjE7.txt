Library Advisory Group Statement of Purpose:



The Library Advisory Group shall serve as an active and collaborative forum between faculty, staff, students and the Library to foster planning, student success, and sustained continuous improvement, in order to uphold the overall mission of SUNY Old Westbury Library. The group will provide an opportunity to give feedback on Library services, facilities, and collections. The advisory group will meet once or twice a semester, as needed. The library values all feedback and will use it to better meet faculty and student needs in the future. 



The Group should consist of:

2 student representatives

1 IT representative

2 faculty members from the School of Art & Science

1 faculty member from the School of Business

1 faculty member from the School of Education

2 faculty members from the Library

Library Director Chair (administrator)
