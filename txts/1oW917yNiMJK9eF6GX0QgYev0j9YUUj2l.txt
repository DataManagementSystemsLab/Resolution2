WHEREAS,    The School of Business recognizes the importance of Healthcare
Management as a large and growing field, and

WHEREAS,    The students would learn legal, operational, financial and
policy affecting Healthcare Management, and

WHEREAS,    While some further resources are needed there will be a synergy
with the other School of Business graduate programs in shared services, and

WHEREAS,    The M.S. program in Healthcare Management as proposed would be
the only 30 credit M.S. on Long Island, and

WHEREAS,    The M.S. program in Healthcare Management as proposed would be
the only offered such program at a public institution on Long Island.


THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED

      That the Senate approves the proposed M.S. in Healthcare Management.

                  Approved by CAP for submission to Senate, October 16, 2015
