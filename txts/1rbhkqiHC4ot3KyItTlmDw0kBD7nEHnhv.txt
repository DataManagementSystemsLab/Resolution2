Resolution on the Faculty Fund
Whereas, faculty funds have been disbursed in an ad hoc manner through out the years;
Therefore Be It Resolved That:
Spending of these funds continue in this manner however giving priority to Faculty needs.
-Rita Colon-Urban

Resolution on the Change of day for Graduation
Rationale
Whereas,

The change in the day of graduation may impose a financial hardship to the families of

the graduates;

And Whereas, There has been no discussion this change with either the Senate or the Faculty at
large;
Therefore Be It Resolved That:

A final decision with regard to the change of day be delayed until a full discussion of the pros and
cons of such a change takes place in the Senate.
-Marie Metlay
